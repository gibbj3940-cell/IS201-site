About the Website

This page explains the design choices, content sources, and assignment requirements addressed by the site.

Design approach

Professional appearance

The website uses Bootstrap for a clean layout, responsive spacing, and consistent navigation. The custom CSS adds a simple navy, blue, white, and gold color palette with strong contrast for readability.

The site avoids distracting effects, animated backgrounds, hard-to-read color combinations, and unnecessary media files. This reduces the chance of broken content and keeps the pages focused on the assignment.

Assignment checklist

  • Professional Bootstrap-based layout
  • Three total HTML pages
  • Clickable page titles and navigation links
  • Résumé included directly in HTML on the index page
  • No broken images, videos, or social icon links
  • Readable colors and consistent spacing

Sources

Data sources used

The statistics on the football page were checked against public college football record sources. The site lists the sources as text instead of adding extra external links, which helps avoid accidental broken links in the submitted project.

  • NCAA.com — college football career rushing leaders
  • College Football at Sports-Reference — career passing, rushing, receiving, and receiving touchdown leaderboards
